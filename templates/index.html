The Growing Threat Landscape

In today's digital world, web applications are the primary target for cybercriminals. Every 39 seconds, there is a new cyber attack somewhere in the world. The question is not IF your application will be attacked, but WHEN.

43% of cyber attacks target small businesses

Many small to medium businesses believe they're too small to be targeted. This false sense of security leaves them vulnerable to automated attacks that scan thousands of websites daily.

Average cost of a data breach: $4.35 Million

Beyond financial loss, breaches result in lost customer trust, legal fees, regulatory fines, and long-term reputation damage that can take years to recover from.

Compliance is Not Optional

GDPR, PCI-DSS, HIPAA, and other regulations require regular security testing. Non-compliance can result in fines up to 4% of annual revenue or €20 million, whichever is higher.
